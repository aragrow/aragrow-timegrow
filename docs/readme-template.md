## About

AutoPrompt is designed for anyone looking to buy an older used car. It leverages AI to analyze listings, check for red flags, and generate a checklist of questions to ask sellers. With AutoPrompt, you can streamline your search and avoid costly mistakes.

---

## Features

- Analyze used car listings for potential issues
- Generate personalized prompts for AI research
- Checklist generator for in-person inspections
- Export results to PDF
